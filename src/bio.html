I am Dante. I believe in understanding worlds and their mechanics, and reflecting the inherent beauty that arises from intricate systems in my work.

I create worlds and define them with my own axioms and design them in my image. It is in the same way inspiring as the whole of a book, the impression of an abstract art work, nature's details together in a landscape.

Naturally that leads to many design fields: computer science, graphic design, statistics, game design, and architecture.

I believe that the second machine revolution does not threaten humanity but rather enlightens us.

However, it requires indefinite retraining and education so long as humans remain humans. Gladly enough, white-collar office jobs involving data and decisions will be the first to go.

The new world will be based on the mastery of machines and algorithms and data, not the exploitation of other humans as is the case for all recorded history.
